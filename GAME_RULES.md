## ðŸ’¡ Strategy Tips

### NOTHING Rounds (1-3)
- Only pairs matter (besides three of a kind)
- High pair is strong
- No flushes/straights = less to worry about
- Consider holding with any pair

### Normal Rounds (4+)
- Straight flushes are rare but powerful
- Flushes and straights become viable
- Three of a kind still beats everything except straight flush
- Weak hands become more risky

### THE DECK
- Most games end via THE DECK showdown
- Being the sole holder is high risk/high reward
- THE DECK uses remaining cards from the shuffled deck
- If pot is huge, consider dropping unless you have a monster hand

### General
- **Early rounds**: Build pot, take calculated risks
- **Big pot**: Be more conservative (losing costs more)
- **Low balance**: Consider riskier plays (you're almost out anyway)
- **Psychology**: Everyone drops when pot is small, more holds when it's big
- **Position**: No advantage - all decisions are simultaneous
